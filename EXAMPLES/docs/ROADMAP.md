### 🚦 Roadmap Rules
1.  **Just-In-Time:** We only write the RFD for milestones in the **NOW** column.
2.  **Transitions:** When a "NOW" milestone meets its **Definition of Done**, the top "NEXT" item is moved to "NOW" to begin its technical design.
3.  **Sync:** Any change to the sequence must be Proposed via a PR and Accepted by the Project Owner.
